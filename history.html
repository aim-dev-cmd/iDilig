Welcome to iDilig: Cultivating Excellence in Pechay Farming


Established in 2023 at the Biñan City Organik Farm in Biñan, Laguna, Philippines, iDilig stands as a revolutionary platform reshaping the landscape of pechay (Chinese cabbage) cultivation. Our mission is to empower farmers and gardening enthusiasts by providing real-time insights into soil moisture and temperature, vital elements for successful crop growth.


At iDilig, simplicity and functionality are paramount. To unlock the full potential of our platform, users are invited to register, ensuring a personalized experience tailored to their unique needs. Once registered, users gain exclusive access to a suite of monitoring features crucial for optimizing pechay growth.


Our core functionality centers around the meticulous observation of soil moisture and temperature levels. Through an intuitive interface, users can access real-time data, review historical trends, and receive timely alerts. This commitment to up-to-the-minute information guarantees that pechay plants are consistently nurtured in the ideal environment for optimal growth.


Leveraging cutting-edge technology, iDilig collects and analyzes data with precision, providing users with actionable insights to make informed decisions about irrigation and cultivation practices. This user-centric approach and integration of technology ensure that iDilig is not just a platform but a partner in the journey towards precision farming and abundant harvests.


Embark on a journey where technology meets cultivation excellence. Join us at iDilig, where innovation and agriculture converge for a brighter, more prosperous future in farming.
